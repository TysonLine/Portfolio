About Me

Hello I'm Tyson Line, a computer science student with a passion for software development, AI, and cybersecurity. I have experience in full-stack development, mobile and web design, fraud detection, and AI-driven applications.

I worked as an ad fraud detection intern at Brave Software, where I analyzed automation scripts used to exploit the Brave Ads reward system and developed a Python-based detection program that identified fraudulent activity by distinguishing human-like mouse movement patterns from automation. Additionally I worked as a mobile app designer at Practice Positivity, where I created intuitive user interfaces, wireframes, and functional requirement documents while researching database solutions for scalability. At Healthy Futures, Ltd., I designed and developed websites, implemented responsive layouts, and created branding materials.

In my projects, I developed a chess engine using bitboards, Zobrist hashing, and an optimized alpha-beta search algorithm, allowing deep search capabilities and UCI compatibility. I also built a therapy robot with speech recognition and NLP, enabling empathetic interactions, and developed full-stack applications using React, TypeScript, Kotlin, Firebase, and MongoDB Atlas. In hackathons, I contributed to AI-driven job boards and real-time database integrations.

I'm always looking for new challenges, whether it's optimizing algorithms, enhancing user experiences, or just a fun project I thought about.
